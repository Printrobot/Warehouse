## Packages
react-webcam | For capturing product and sticker photos
html5-qrcode | For scanning QR codes (orders, locations)
recharts | For admin dashboard statistics
framer-motion | For smooth transitions between wizard steps and page loads
clsx | Utility for conditional classes
tailwind-merge | Utility for merging tailwind classes

## Notes
The app uses a "simulate" approach for OCR and some hardware features.
Camera integration uses `react-webcam`.
QR Scanning uses `html5-qrcode`.
Theme should be high-contrast and "industrial" (large buttons for gloved hands).
